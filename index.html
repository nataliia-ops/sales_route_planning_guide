🤖 What Happens Automatically
1
Collects your stores
Reads your complete store database with all dates, visit times, and excluded days
2
Prioritises by urgency
Overdue → specific date this week → this month → unvisited → oldest last visit
3
Places appointment anchors NEW
Stores with a visit_time are fixed first. The rest of the day is built around them.
4
Groups by region
Clusters stores by postal code. Fills gaps around appointments with geographically close stores.
5
Sends plan by email at 4pm
Google Sheet link + list of all appointments with fixed times for the week
🎯 Store Priority Order
1️⃣
Overdue — past their scheduled date, not visited since
2️⃣

3️⃣
Month-due — "maart"/"march" during that month
4️⃣

5️⃣
Longest since last visit — oldest first
6️⃣
Geographically close — tiebreaker

📅 Month-Based Planning

Set next: march → planned during March. If not planned by the last week of March, it's guaranteed — even if the day is full.

✅ You'll never miss a store scheduled for a specific month.
⚙️ Sparse Day Auto-fill NEW

If a day has fewer than 70% of capacity planned, the system auto-selects nearby stores to fill it.

💡 You'll see ⚙️ Auto-filled: X stores in your morning email. You can still adjust the sheet before leaving.

Important Rules
Key guidelines to keep the system working correctly
⚠️ Store Name Consistency

Names must match between Slack, Excel, and Odoo. Small typos are OK — chain + location must be recognisable.

✅ These all match:
Client: Apotheek Walema
Client: APOTHEEK WALEMA
Client: walema mechelen
❌ These won't match:
Client: Walema ← too short
Client: De Walema ← different
💡 When in doubt, copy the exact name from your Excel sheet.
🔄 Smart Chain Matching
AD = DELHAIZE
"AD Hippodroom" matches "Delhaize Hippodroom"
GB = CARREFOUR
Historical alias recognised automatically
📅 next: Field — Critical
Without next:, the store will NOT be automatically replanned.

✅ Will be replanned

Client: Apotheek Walema
next: april

❌ Will NOT be replanned

Client: Apotheek Walema
note: Good visit
← no next: field
